Napoleon's life

  • 1769- Born
  • 1799- Following a coup d'etat, Napoleon becomes First Consul of the new French government
  • 1814- Napoleon is exiled to Elba; his wife and son take refuge in Vienna
  • 1815- Louis XVIII flees, Napoleon takes control, begins "Hundred Days" campaign
  • 1815- Napoleon abdicates throne for the second time
  • 1821- Napoleon dies
